## About Me

Hi, I am **Ahmed Mustayeen**, a CSE student and developer interested in the space where **machine learning**, **full-stack engineering**, and **real-world student tools** meet.

I enjoy building projects that are useful, readable, and practical: from AI-powered academic planning systems to web platforms for university life and deep learning experiments for music generation.

- Currently exploring **AI/ML, generative systems, and full-stack product development**
- Building with **Python, TypeScript, JavaScript, PHP, EJS, and Jupyter**
- Interested in **education technology, creative AI, and applied machine learning**
- Goal: turn strong ideas into polished, documented, demo-ready projects
